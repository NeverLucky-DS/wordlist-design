Card — a sheet of raised white paper; the default lifted container.

```jsx
<Card elevation="lg" radius="xl">…</Card>
```
- `elevation`: `flat` · `sm` · `md` (default) · `lg` · `sheet` (the premium floating popover).
- `radius`: `lg` · `xl` (default) · `2xl` (the writing surface). `pad` toggles 24px inner padding.
